MOD IS INITIALLY BUILT UPON THE CLASSICAL VERSION OF MILLENNIUM DAWN, AS WELL AS USING ASSETS FROM THE NEW ONE, ALL CREDIT TO THEM

The Information Age brings to you a (hopefully) immesive geopolitical simulator starting from 2018, aiming to balance sandbox and realism.

These DLC's are recommended while playing (not required): Together for Victory, Death or Dishonor, Waking the Tiger, Man the Guns
Features:
Map fully reworked
Vast amount of 3D Unit and Building models
Large amount of flavor events, including Eurovision
Syrian Civil War
Somali Civil War
Libyan Civil War
Yemeni Civil War
Geopolitical events, such as the Prespa Agreement
Extremely buffed nukes as a deterrent to nuclear war
Other fundamental game changes
